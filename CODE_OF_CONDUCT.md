# Code of Conduct for Lumin

## 1. Introduction

This Code of Conduct (CoC) describes our expectations from all members of the Lumin community, as well as the steps that need to be taken to address any violations. We strive to create an open, welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of their background, gender, gender, sexual orientation, disability, personal experience, appearance, race, ethnicity, religion or any other characteristics.

## 2. Our Commitment

As participants and leaders of this project, we commit to making participation in our community a harassment-free experience, regardless of gender, gender, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, size, race, religion, or experience.

We are committed to making our community open and welcoming to all. Examples of behaviors that contribute to creating a positive environment:

* Using friendly and inclusive language.
* Respect for different points of view and experience.
 Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
* Focus on what is best for the community.
* Showing empathy for other participants.

## 3. Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* The use of sexualized language or images, as well as unwanted sexual attention.
 Trolling, offensive/humiliating comments, personal or political attacks, as well as attacks.
* Public or private harassment.
* Posting other people's personal information, such as a physical or email address, without explicit permission.
* Other behavior that may reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional environment.
* Showing disrespect to other participants, including disregard for their opinions, experience, or time.
* Using aggressive or offensive language.
* Creating a hostile atmosphere, whether intentionally or unintentionally.

## 4. Our Responsibilities

The responsibility for maintaining this code of conduct lies with the project organizers. The project organizers have the right and are responsible for deleting, editing or rejecting comments, commits, code, wiki edits and other contributions that do not comply with this code of conduct, as well as for temporarily or permanently banning any participant for behavior that is considered inappropriate, threatening, offensive or harmful.

## 5. Application

Cases of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior can be reported by contacting the Lumin team via: [Provide contact information, such as email or links to issue tracker, list of moderators]. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and this will lead to the necessary response appropriate to the circumstances. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with respect to the person who reported the violation.

When applying the Code of Conduct, the organizers will be guided by the principles of:

* **Impartiality:** We will treat all communications fairly and impartially.
* **Proportionality:** We will apply measures appropriate to the severity of the violation.
* **Privacy:** We will respect the confidentiality of the parties.

## 6. Appeal

If you experience any violations, please contact us immediately. If you witness a violation, please report it.

We look forward to your participation and look forward to your cooperation in creating a positive, safe and inclusive environment for everyone.

## 7. Changes

This code of conduct may be changed from time to time. All changes will be posted on this resource.
